WHAT IS SUSTAINABLE LIVING?

Sustainable living is a way of life that aims to reduce the environmental impact of individuals and society. It involves making conscious choices to minimize the use of natural resources, reduce waste and pollution, and support socio-economic development.

SUSTAINABLE LIVING VS ZERO WASTE

Sustainable living and zero-waste living are both lifestyle choices that aim to reduce environmental impact, but zero-waste living focuses more on reducing the amount of trash produced.

Sustainable Living

Involves making choices that are good for the environment, such as reducing resource use, eating locally, choosing organic options, and using clean energy sources. Sustainable living is a broader concept than zero-waste living, as it considers the entire impact of a person's choices on the environment.

Zero-waste Living

Involves creating as little waste as possible by avoiding single-use items, reusing, recycling, and composting. Zero-waste living also involves rethinking how products and materials are consumed and disposed of.

IMPACT OF SUSTAINABLE LIVING

Sustainable living has many positive impacts on the environment, society, and individuals, including:

Protecting the environment: Sustainable living practices can help preserve natural resources, reduce the environmental impact of clothing production, and mitigate climate change.

Improving health and well-being: Sustainable living can lead to improved health and well-being.

Reducing energy costs and Increasing renewable energy sources: Sustainable living can reduce energy costs and can lead to increased renewable energy sources.

Improving water and air quality: Sustainable actions can lead to improved water and air quality.

Improving life quality: Sustainable development can improve life quality.

Composting is a natural process that turns organic waste, like food scraps and yard clippings, into a nutrient-rich soil amendment called compost. Composting allows organic material to decompose with the help of microorganisms, fungi, and insects in a controlled environment. This process creates a dark, earthy material that can enrich soil, improve its structure, and help plants grow.

Reduce,Recyle,Reuse and Recover. The 4Rs - Reduce, Reuse, Recycle, and Recover - is a simple way for us all to make a big impact. These four simple actions can help us keep our environment cleaner. SUSTAINABLE DEVELOPMENT GOALS - SDG

The Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) aim to transform our world. They are a call to action to end poverty and inequality, protect the planet, and ensure that all people enjoy health, justice and prosperity. It is critical that no one is left behind.

The 17 SDGs

Goal 1

End poverty in all its forms everywhere.

Goal 2

End hunger, achieve food security and improved nutrition and promote sustainable agriculture.

Goal 3

Ensure healthy lives and promote well-being for all at all ages.

Goal 4

Ensure inclusive and equitable quality education and promote lifelong learning opportunities for all.

Goal 5

Achieve gender equality and empower all women and girls.

Goal 6

Ensure access to safe water sources and sanitation for all.

Goal 7

Ensure access to affordable, reliable, sustainable and modern energy for all

Goal 8

Promote sustained, inclusive and sustainable economic growth, full and productive employment and decent work for all

Goal 9

Build resilient infrastructure, promote inclusive and sustainable industrialization and foster innovation

Goal 10

Reduce inequality within and among countries

Goal 11

Make cities and human settlements inclusive, safe, resilient and sustainable

Goal 12

Ensure sustainable consumption and production patterns

Goal 13

Take urgent action to combat climate change and its impacts

Goal 14

Conserve and sustainably use the oceans, seas and marine resources for sustainable development

Goal 15

Protect, restore and promote sustainable use of terrestrial ecosystems, sustainably manage forests, combat desertification, and halt and reverse land degradation and halt biodiversity loss

Goal 16

Promote peaceful and inclusive societies for sustainable development, provide access to justice for all and build effective, accountable and inclusive institutions at all levels

Goal 17

Strengthen the means of implementation and revitalize the Global Partnership for Sustainable Development
